Gorillas can be tracked year-round, although the rainy season makes for more challenging treks. There are two rainy seasons in Uganda, one from March to May and another from October to November. There are benefits to visiting during the rainy seasons, but the rains do make for tougher treks. All gorilla trekkers need to obtain a permit from the Uganda Wildlife Authority. These current rates for permits is $700 per person at time of writing, and while they used to be cheaper in the rainy season, this is no longer the case.
